# AI 口语练习话题库

> 每天选一个话题，用英文和 AI 对话 30~60 分钟。
> 不要怕犯错，重点是"说出来"。

---

## 第一阶段话题（简单，有话说）

### 日常生活
1. Talk about your morning routine
2. What did you eat today? Describe it in detail
3. Describe your workspace / desk setup
4. What's the weather like today? How does it affect your mood?
5. Talk about your commute to work
6. What do you usually do on weekends?
7. Describe your favorite restaurant
8. What's your ideal day off?

### 技术（你的舒适区）
9. Explain what you're working on right now (in simple English)
10. What programming language do you use and why?
11. Describe a bug you recently fixed
12. What's your opinion on AI coding assistants?
13. How do you organize your code?
14. What's the most interesting project you've worked on?
15. Explain REST API to a non-technical person
16. What's your development environment like?

### 观点（简单表达）
17. Do you prefer working from home or office? Why?
18. What's your favorite app and why?
19. Morning person or night owl?
20. What's one thing you'd like to learn this year?

## 第二阶段话题（需要更多表达）

### 技术深度
21. Explain the difference between SQL and NoSQL databases
22. What is microservices architecture? Pros and cons?
23. How would you design a URL shortener?
24. What's your opinion on TypeScript vs JavaScript?
25. Explain CI/CD to someone new to software development
26. What makes a good API?
27. How do you handle technical debt?
28. Describe your ideal software architecture

### 工作相关
29. How do you handle disagreements with teammates about technical decisions?
30. Describe your team's development process
31. How do you estimate task complexity?
32. What would you do if you inherited a legacy codebase?
33. How do you stay updated with new technologies?
34. Describe a time you had to learn a new technology quickly

### 日常深入
35. What's happening in AI right now? What excites you?
36. Talk about a documentary or video you recently watched
37. What are your career goals for the next 3 years?
38. If you could live anywhere in the world, where and why?
39. What's one habit that changed your life?
40. What do you think about remote work culture?

## 第三阶段话题（高级表达和辩论）

### 观点辩论
41. Is AI going to replace programmers? Why or why not?
42. Should all software be open source?
43. Is a computer science degree still worth it?
44. Privacy vs convenience — where do you draw the line?
45. Is the tech industry in a bubble?
46. Should developers learn to manage or stay technical?

### 技术演讲（模拟）
47. Give a 5-minute presentation on a technical topic of your choice
48. Explain a system design problem as if presenting to your team
49. Describe your career journey so far
50. Pitch a product idea in 3 minutes

### 生活哲学
51. What does "success" mean to you?
52. How has your perspective on work changed over the years?
53. What's the best advice you've ever received?
54. If you could go back 10 years, what would you tell yourself?
55. What role does technology play in your life?

## 使用方法

### 和 ChatGPT Voice 对话时：
```
开场白（复制粘贴）：
I want to practice my English speaking. Let's talk about [话题]. 
Please correct my grammar and suggest better expressions when I make mistakes. 
Keep the conversation natural and flowing.
```

### 和 Claude 文字对话时：
```
Let's have a conversation in English about [话题]. 
Please:
1. Keep the conversation flowing naturally
2. Correct my grammar mistakes
3. Suggest more natural expressions when mine sound awkward
4. Ask follow-up questions to keep me talking
```

### 每日记录：
对话结束后，记录：
- 今天学到了哪些新表达？
- 哪些地方卡住了？为什么？
- 明天想练习什么？
